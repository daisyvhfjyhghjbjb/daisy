Required Documents & Criteria

General Requirements:

  • Official transcripts from previous academic institutions.
  • Copy of National ID / Passport.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
  • Proof of English Language Proficiency (e.g., IELTS/TOEFL) for international students.

Program-Specific Requirements:

  • Portfolio: Required for most Fine Art, Graphic Design, Digital Animation, and Fashion Design programs. (Specific guidelines provided per program).
  • Audition/Interview: Required for Performing Arts and Music programs.
  • Statement of Purpose/Personal Essay: Required for Degree and Master programs.
  • Letters of Recommendation: Usually required for Master programs.

For detailed program-specific requirements, please refer to the Programs page or contact our admissions office.
