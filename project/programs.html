Our Academic Programs

Explore a diverse range of artistic disciplines and find your path to creative mastery.

Foundation Programs

Our Foundation programs provide a strong artistic base, ideal for aspiring students looking to build fundamental skills and explore various art forms.

Foundation in Visual Arts

Develop core skills in drawing, painting, sculpture, and digital art basics.

Foundation in Performing Arts

An introduction to basic acting, dance techniques, music theory, and stagecraft.

Foundation in Digital Media

Learn graphic design essentials, animation principles, and fundamental video editing.

Diploma Programs

Diploma programs offer specialized training, allowing students to deepen their knowledge and practical abilities in a specific artistic discipline.

Diploma in Fine Art

Focus on advanced drawing, painting techniques, printmaking, and art history.

Diploma in Graphic Design

Covers branding, typography, web design principles, and illustration techniques.

Diploma in Digital Animation

Explore character design, 2D/3D animation workflows, and storytelling through animation.

Diploma in Music Performance

Intensive instrumental/vocal training, music composition, and ensemble performance.

Diploma in Theatre Arts

Develop acting techniques, directing skills, playwriting, and stage management expertise.

Degree Programs (BA Hons)

Our Bachelor of Arts (Honours) programs provide comprehensive education, fostering critical thinking, advanced skills, and preparation for professional careers.

BA (Hons) Fine Art

Intensive studio practice, critical theory, exhibition design, and specialization opportunities.

BA (Hons) Graphic Communication

Advanced design projects, UX/UI design, advertising strategies, and portfolio development.

BA (Hons) Digital Arts & Animation

Master advanced 3D modeling, visual effects, game design concepts, and interactive media.

BA (Hons) Music & Sound Design

Learn music production, audio engineering, film scoring, and sound art creation.

BA (Hons) Performing Arts

Advanced performance techniques, choreography, directing, and arts management principles.

BA (Hons) Fashion Design

Covers garment construction, textile design, fashion illustration, and collection development.

BA (Hons) Creative Writing

Focus on fiction, poetry, screenwriting, and understanding the publishing industry.

Master Programs (MA)

Master of Arts programs offer advanced study and research opportunities, ideal for professionals seeking leadership roles or further academic pursuits.

MA Fine Art Practice

Engage in research-based studio practice, curatorial studies, and advanced art theory.

MA Visual Communication

Explore advanced design methodologies, in-depth design research, and brand strategy.

MA Digital Media Arts

Focus on experimental animation, interactive installations, and virtual reality development.

MA Musicology & Composition

Delve into advanced music theory, ethnomusicology, and contemporary composition.

MA Arts Management & Cultural Policy

Study arts administration, cultural entrepreneurship, and policy analysis for the arts sector.
