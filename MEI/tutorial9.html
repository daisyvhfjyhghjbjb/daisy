Mickey Mouse is one of the most iconic and enduring characters in animation history. Created by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks, Mickey made his first appearance in 1928. Over the years, Mickey has evolved from a mischievous character into a symbol of optimism, curiosity, and kindness. He represents the spirit of adventure and is often seen tackling new challenges with courage and cheer. From theme parks to merchandise and TV shows, Mickey remains the face of the Disney brand worldwide.

Goofy, with his clumsy yet lovable personality, has brought laughter to audiences for decades. He is known for his tall frame, distinctive voice, and positive attitude. Despite often getting into trouble due to his goofiness, he always manages to solve problems in the most unexpected and entertaining ways. Goofy shows that it's okay to be different and that happiness can be found in simplicity.

Minnie Mouse is graceful, stylish, and warm-hearted. As Mickey’s longtime companion, she has been a symbol of love and loyalty since her first appearance. Minnie’s charm lies in her gentle manner and timeless fashion sense, often wearing her signature polka-dotted dress and bow. She inspires kindness and shows that being sweet is a strength.

Pluto is Mickey’s loyal dog and a fan favorite. Though he doesn't speak, Pluto's expressive face and physical humor make him one of the most relatable characters. He often gets into playful trouble, but his loyalty to Mickey never wavers. Pluto's stories are filled with warmth and fun, appealing to kids and adults alike.

Daisy Duck, the stylish and confident counterpart to Donald Duck, is full of sass and charm. She’s not afraid to speak her mind and often takes the lead in any situation. Daisy adds sophistication to the Disney gang and teaches us to be confident in who we are. Her balance of elegance and assertiveness makes her a beloved character across generations.
